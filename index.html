Phone Addiction has become one of our generation's most widespread issues, and that's by design.

Social media algorithms and other apps are designed to give you constant hits of dopamine.

That's non-stop instant gratification.

The result is neurologically similar to substance addiction.

Know the Facts!

  1. The percentage of teenagers displaying symptoms of anxiety or depression in the past 2 weeks was higher among those with 4+ hours of daily screen time.
  2. Rather than giving us true pleasure, little hits of dopamine motivate us to keep doing things in search of that true satisfaction. That’s why we just scroll endlessly.
  3. Studies have shown that about 25% of individuals have experienced or are currently dealing with mobile phone addiction. Among them, 34.9% of mobile phone users are between the ages of 18 and 30.
  4. Blue light exposure during non-daylight hours suppresses melatonin, a vital sleep hormone. Even one less hour of sleep per night can lead to decreased attention span, lower energy, mood changes, and more.

The power to unplug is in your hands!

Moderating screentime can lead to a happier and healthier life!

Less time looking at screens can lead to:

  • Improved mental health
  • An improved sleep schedule
  • Improved academic performance
  • Greater social connectivity
